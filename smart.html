Company Mission

Provide Loan services to applicants and funding institutions professionally using best practices of industry with competitive costing, efficiency and scale up using technology.

Problem Faced by the industry

Average Housing loan process time takes more than a month. 30-40% of the applications ends up in rejection due to bank assessment on application or applicant unwilling to take the reduced loan amount or not satisfied with loan product. Due to this, projects get delayed and the supply chain gets halted. Processing fee will be lost in case of rejection, unprofessional handling of applicants by the agents as the application is being sourced by agents who lack professional expertise. Lack of options for the customers on which bank to apply as only after a full process will they be knowing about the rejection, due to this applicants have to apply the process all over again to another institution. Funding institutes covertly announces hidden charges in the last minute , due to project funding pressure, applicant has to accept the offer.

LoanSMart Solution

LoanSMart Provides end to end solution for Loan Application requirements from eligibility calculation and coordinate with bank/NBFC reps for best offer based on preliminary documentation and track the progress technologically ensuring transparency.

Founder Vision

Our product is developed based on concept which will add value Mainly to the housing sector end users who are regular middle class people in general who needs timely financial help to bridge the commitment to purchase/construct their dream home by reducing cost of finance, timely Disbursement of funds and possibly catalyze the growth of the sector and benefit all the stake holders.
